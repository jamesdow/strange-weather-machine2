## Cross-Platform Patterns

1. **The strongest transferable format is "proof before polish."** TikTok rewards real-life product usage and full watches/skips; YouTube hauls perform when they show try-on proof; Pinterest performs when inspiration becomes a usable collage or shoppable decision. [https://newsroom.tiktok.com/introducing-tiktok-next-2026-our-trend-forecast-for-marketers-for-the-year-ahead?_hsmi=399120832&lang=en] [HIGH]; [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u77Jag1KqD8] [MEDIUM]; [https://create.pinterest.com/en-my/success-stories/primark/] [HIGH] Implication: Primark should make product proof the first frame, not the last claim.

2. **Price shock is a platform-native beat, but the mechanic changes by channel.** TikTok uses "find/haul/viral" urgency; Instagram uses shareable double-take; Pinterest uses shoppable trend-worlds; YouTube uses detailed evaluation. [https://tiktokhashtags.com/hashtag/primark/] [MEDIUM]; [https://corporate.primark.com/en-gb/a/news/corporate-news/shockingly-chic-primark-unveils-new-womenswear-campaign] [HIGH]; [https://corporate.primark.com/en-gb/a/news/corporate-news/pinterest-and-primark-launch-new-ways-to-shop-trend-inspired-collection] [HIGH]

3. **The audience journey likely runs from TikTok/Instagram discovery to YouTube/Pinterest validation.** TikTok's own trend forecast says shoppers compare and validate from trusted creators before committing; Pinterest says Gen Z uses it for product/brand information and final decisions; YouTube haul titles show post-discovery detail seeking. [https://newsroom.tiktok.com/introducing-tiktok-next-2026-our-trend-forecast-for-marketers-for-the-year-ahead?_hsmi=399120832&lang=en] [HIGH]; [https://business.pinterest.com/audience/?change_language=true] [HIGH]; [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wzsWlsBzfYM] [MEDIUM] Implication: do not judge every platform by direct conversion; design handoffs.

4. **Brand-created social is strongest when it behaves like community infrastructure.** Primark's #Primania rules invite public Instagram posts into a UGC gallery; SocialChain discusses operationalising colleague-generated content; Pinterest Collages invite users to build their own looks. [https://www.primark.com/en-us/primania-gallery-rules] [HIGH]; [https://www.linkedin.com/posts/socialchain-agency_socialminds-x-primark-activity-7448024959341346816-R-wW] [MEDIUM]; [https://create.pinterest.com/en-my/success-stories/primark/] [HIGH] Implication: Primark should scale formats that let shoppers and colleagues do the proving.

What the evidence does NOT show: It does not prove exact cross-platform conversion paths at user level; that requires analytics attribution.
